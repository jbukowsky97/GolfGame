How to Play:

Goal

Get the ball into all three holes in as few strokes as possble

Winning

Win by scoring under par

Movement

Move your character with WASD

Switch Clubs

Switch clubs with numbers 1 - 4 (Driver, Iron, Wedge, Putter)

Gameplay

1. Tee Ball

Tee the ball up anywhere in the current hole's teebox by pressing E

2. Address Ball

Once close enough, you can address the ball with E

3. Hit Ball

Hold E to perform your backswing and release to start your downswing. The larger your backswing the further the ball will go

4. Repeat

Repeat Steps 2 & 3 until you get the ball into the hole, and then head to the next tee box and re-tee
